Cohora FAQ

How do Brands migrate their customers from their CRM to the Community?
1.In Notification - you have to verify or will need to configure one-off email message for the customers and also as an incentive give the customer xxx amount of loyalty coins when they signup up.
2.In loyalty setup - select the event and attribute the correct amount of Loyalty coins for first-time login.
3.On Brand Admi, click on Customer View, and navigate to Import Customers.
4.Download the Cohora file format, use this format to upload all your CRM contact.
5.Use custom fields to add any new additional fields example sales history, last product purchase.
6.Upload files by clicking on the choose file button.
7.Profile insert mode - select the most appropriate filter.
8.Send invite email to customers - select most appropriate “select to all customers in excel sheet”.
9.The system will automatically create a pre-login and email the customer directly once you click on the import customers button.
10.Use Thoughspot to track who has clicked and who has a login, who has visited the site
a.Click on ThoughtSpot under the Insight category, add your username and password, and analyze the data by dragging the attributes, dimensions, and metrics.
11.If Brand want, options - they can set up a reminder campaign to target these users.
12.Pre setup engagement activities for the new users to nurture them through the adoption phase such as a chatroom, quiz, a contest for them, some unique offer…
